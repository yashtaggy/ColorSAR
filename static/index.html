ColorSAR

Artificial Intelligence for Synthetic Aperture Radar Image Colorization

ColorSAR is an advanced AI system designed to transform monochromatic SAR imagery into realistic optical-style color data. Utilizing a Deep Ensemble Decoder (combining ResNet-50 and DenseNet-121) and trained via Conditional GANs, it meticulously predicts spectral channels while retaining 100% of the original radar texture.
